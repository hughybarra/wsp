Reservations

We accept reservations for all party sizes on Sundays Thru Thursdays. It is “first come-first serve” on Fridays and Saturdays unless you are a group of 8 people or more in which case we take limited reservations with our "Personalized Party Menu"! Please contact a Reservation Specialist at (914) 637-3737 or email us at reservationsnr@doncoqui.com to make your special arrangements.
